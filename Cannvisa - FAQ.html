Cannvisa FAQs

Cannvisa is a trusted provider of premium medical cannabis products, offering a carefully curated selection of oils, edibles, and therapeutic formulations. We combine pharmaceutical expertise with compassionate care to serve patients across Germany.

Our product range includes:
  • Medical-grade cannabis flowers
  • THC/CBD oils and tinctures
  • Edibles and capsules
  • Topical creams and balms
  • Vaporization products
All products are sourced from licensed producers and meet strict quality standards.

Currently, we primarily serve patients within Germany. For international orders, we can ship to certain EU countries where medical cannabis is legal. Please contact our support team with your location details for specific shipping information.

1. Prescription Submission

Upload your valid medical cannabis prescription through our secure portal.

2. Pharmacy Processing

Our licensed partner pharmacy verifies your prescription and prepares your order within 24 hours.

3. DHL Shipping

Your package is shipped via DHL with temperature-controlled packaging when required.

4. Tracking & Delivery

You'll receive a tracking number for real-time updates. Delivery typically takes 2-3 business days within Germany.

Orders placed after 2pm or on weekends will be processed the next business day.
